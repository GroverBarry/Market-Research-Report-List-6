<p><p>The Sign Language Interpretation System market includes three main types: Speech Recognition, Camera Recognition, and Contact Identification. Speech Recognition systems use technology to interpret spoken words into sign language. Camera Recognition systems use visual input to recognize and interpret sign language gestures. Contact Identification systems involve wearable devices that track and interpret sign language movements. Each type serves the purpose of facilitating communication between deaf or hard of hearing individuals and those who do not understand sign language.</p></p>
