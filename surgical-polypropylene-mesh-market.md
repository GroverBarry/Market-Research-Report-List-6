<p><p>Surgical polypropylene mesh comes in two main types: non-absorbable and absorbable. Non-absorbable surgical mesh is designed to provide permanent support for tissue repair, commonly used in hernia repair surgeries. Absorbable surgical mesh, on the other hand, is intended to degrade and be absorbed by the body over time, typically used in temporary support applications like in pelvic organ prolapse repair. Both types are crucial in various surgical procedures to provide structural support and facilitate tissue healing.</p></p>
