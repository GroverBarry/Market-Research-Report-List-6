<p><p>Semiconductor thinner market consists of two main types: Positive Photoresist Removal and Negative Photoresist Removal. Positive Photoresist Removal involves the removal of areas coated with photoresist material that are exposed to light, while Negative Photoresist Removal involves the removal of areas coated with photoresist material that are not exposed to light. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor fabrication process by allowing for precise patterning of circuitry on semiconductor wafers, ultimately enabling the creation of advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
