<p><p>Waterproof fabrics market can be divided into two main types: Breathable Waterproof Fabrics and Non-Breathable Waterproof Fabrics. Breathable fabrics allow for air and moisture to pass through, making them comfortable for wearers engaging in physical activities. Non-Breathable fabrics, on the other hand, do not allow air or moisture to pass through, making them ideal for extreme wet conditions where keeping dry is the main priority. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for waterproof fabrics.</p></p>
