Toilet flush valves are used to control the flow of water from the tank to the bowl, while handles are used to initiate the flushing process. Toilet flappers create a seal to prevent water from leaking into the bowl between flushes, and toilet seals are used to prevent leaks between the toilet and the floor. These products are essential for maintaining the functionality and efficiency of toilets.</p></p>
