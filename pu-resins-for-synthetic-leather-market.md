<p><strong>The PU Resins for Synthetic Leather Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>PU resins are commonly used in the manufacturing of synthetic leather through two main processes: dry-process and wet-process. </p><p>Dry-process synthetic leather involves coating a fabric substrate with PU resin and then curing it through heat treatment, resulting in a more durable and lightweight material. </p><p>On the other hand, wet-process synthetic leather involves directly impregnating a fabric substrate with PU resin solutions and then curing it through various chemical processes, resulting in a softer and more flexible material. Both processes have their own unique characteristics and applications in the synthetic leather market.</p></p>
