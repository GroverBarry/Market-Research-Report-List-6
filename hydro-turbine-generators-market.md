<p><strong>The Hydro Turbine Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Hydro Turbine Generator</li><li>Reaction Hydro Turbine Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydro Turbine Generators Market includes two main types: Impulse Hydro Turbine Generators and Reaction Hydro Turbine Generators. Impulse turbines convert the kinetic energy of water into mechanical energy by directing water jets onto buckets attached to the turbine wheel. Reaction turbines, on the other hand, convert both kinetic and potential energy of water into mechanical energy by utilizing the pressure of water flow. Both types of turbines play a crucial role in generating electricity from hydro power plants, providing sustainable and clean energy sources.</p></p>
