<p><p>Food Grade rPET, also known as recycled polyethylene terephthalate, is commonly available in two main forms: pellet and flake. Pellets are small, cylindrical pieces of rPET that are typically used in the manufacturing of new food grade packaging materials. Flakes, on the other hand, are small, irregularly shaped pieces of rPET that are often used in the production of fibers for textiles and other non-packaging applications. Both pellet and flake markets play a crucial role in the sustainable recycling of plastic waste and the reduction of reliance on virgin plastics.</p></p>
