<p><p>Anti-sway systems are used in different industries to prevent excessive motion of equipment like cranes and vehicles. There are two main types of anti-sway systems - hydraulic and mechanical. Hydraulic anti-sway systems use fluid pressure to dampen motion and maintain stability, while mechanical anti-sway systems use mechanical components like springs and dampers. Both systems are designed to improve safety and efficiency by reducing swaying and swinging during movement, ultimately increasing stability and control.</p></p>
