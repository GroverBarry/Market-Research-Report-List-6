<p><p>Process chillers are used in industrial settings to cool process equipment or machinery. There are two main types of process chillers in the market - air cooled and water cooled. Air cooled chillers use ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for areas where water availability is limited. On the other hand, water cooled chillers use water as a cooling medium, providing more efficient cooling compared to air cooled chillers. Companies can choose the type of chiller based on their specific cooling requirements and environmental factors.</p></p>
