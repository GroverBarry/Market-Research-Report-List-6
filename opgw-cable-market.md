<p><ul><li>Layer Stranding Structure</li><li>Central Tube Design</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OPGW Cable Market can be classified into two main types based on their design - Layer Stranding Structure and Central Tube Design. Layer Stranding Structure cables consist of multiple layers of metallic wires wrapped around the optical fibers for added strength and protection. Central Tube Design cables, on the other hand, have a central tube that houses the optical fibers with metallic wires around it. Each design has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
