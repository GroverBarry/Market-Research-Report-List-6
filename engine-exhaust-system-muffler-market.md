<p><strong>The Engine Exhaust System Muffler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Exhaust Muffler</li><li>Reactive Exhaust Muffler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Absorption exhaust mufflers are designed to reduce engine noise by using sound-absorbing materials such as fiberglass or foam. They work by absorbing sound waves created by the engine and converting them into heat. On the other hand, reactive exhaust mufflers use chambers and baffles to reflect and cancel out sound waves, reducing noise levels. Both types of mufflers are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications to comply with noise regulations and improve overall driving experience.</p></p>
