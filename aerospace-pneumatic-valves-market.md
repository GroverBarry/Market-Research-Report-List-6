<p><p>Aerospace pneumatic valves are categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. Poppet valves are commonly used for controlling the flow of gas in aircraft systems. Pilot valves are used to regulate the pressure in pneumatic systems. Flapper-nozzle valves are designed for precise control and high flow rates. Ball and plug valves offer reliable shut-off and minimal leakage. Other types of aerospace pneumatic valves include butterfly valves, diaphragm valves, and gate valves, each serving specific purposes in aircraft systems.</p></p>
