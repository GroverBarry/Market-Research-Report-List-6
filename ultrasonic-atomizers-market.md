<p><p>Ultrasonic atomizers are classified into various types based on their spray pattern, flow direction, and operating temperature. Wide spray ultrasonic atomizers produce a wide dispersion of particles, while narrow spray atomizers produce a concentrated stream. Radial flow atomizers have a circular flow path, and high operating temperature atomizers are designed to withstand extreme heat. Each type caters to specific applications and requirements, offering diverse solutions for industries such as healthcare, agriculture, and electronics.</p></p>
