<p><p>Contact adhesives market can be categorized into three main types: CR (chloroprene rubber), SBS (styrene-butadiene-styrene), and Others. CR adhesives are known for their high heat resistance and good bonding properties. SBS adhesives offer excellent flexibility and adhesion on a variety of surfaces. The "Others" category includes adhesives made from different materials such as polyurethane and epoxy. These adhesives cater to specific requirements and applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and packaging, providing a wide range of options for different bonding needs.</p></p>
