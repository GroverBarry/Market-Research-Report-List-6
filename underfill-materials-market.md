<p><strong>The Underfill Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Underfill Material (CUF)</li><li>No Flow Underfill Material (NUF)</li><li>Molded Underfill Material (MUF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfill materials are used in semiconductor packaging to improve the reliability of electronic devices by providing mechanical support and preventing stress-induced damage. There are three main types of underfill materials on the market: Capillary Underfill Material (CUF) which flows into the gap between the chip and substrate through capillary action, No Flow Underfill Material (NUF) which is pre-applied and does not flow during curing, and Molded Underfill Material (MUF) which is injected into the gap and cured in place.</p></p>
