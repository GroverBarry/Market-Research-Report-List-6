<p><strong>The Horizontal Batch Mixer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Shaft</li><li>Twin Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal batch mixers are divided into two main types: Single Shaft and Twin Shaft mixers. Single Shaft mixers typically have a single mixing shaft that rotates to combine materials. They are commonly used for dry mixing applications. On the other hand, Twin Shaft mixers have two mixing shafts that work together to thoroughly blend materials. They are often used for wet and sticky materials which require more intensive mixing. Both Single Shaft and Twin Shaft mixers are popular choices in industries such as food processing, construction, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
