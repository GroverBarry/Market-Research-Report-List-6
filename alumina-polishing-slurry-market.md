<p><p>Alumina polishing slurry is available in two main types: Nano grade and Micron grade. Nano grade slurry consists of ultra-fine particles, typically less than 100 nanometers in size, which can achieve high precision polishing for advanced electronic devices and optical components. On the other hand, Micron grade slurry contains larger particles, typically between 1-10 micrometers in size, and is used for more general polishing applications in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing and metal finishing. Both grades offer different levels of precision and efficiency in polishing processes.</p></p>
