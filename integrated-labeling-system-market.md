<p><p>Integrated Labeling System market includes three main types: Manual Labeling, Semi-automatic Labeling, and Automatic Labeling. Manual Labeling involves operators manually applying labels to products. Semi-automatic Labeling utilizes machines to partially automate the process, reducing labor and increasing efficiency. Automatic Labeling fully automates the process, with machines applying labels without the need for human intervention. Each type offers varying levels of speed, accuracy, and cost-effectiveness, catering to the diverse needs of different businesses in the labeling industry.</p></p>
