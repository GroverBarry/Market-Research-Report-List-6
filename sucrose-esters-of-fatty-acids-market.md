<p><ul><li>High HLB (Above 9)</li><li>Medium HLB (7-9)</li><li>Low HLB (Below 6)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sucrose esters of fatty acids are categorized into three types based on their Hydrophilic-Lipophilic Balance (HLB) values. High HLB (Above 9) sucrose esters are more water-soluble and emulsify well in polar solvents. Medium HLB (7-9) sucrose esters have a balanced affinity towards water and oil, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. Low HLB (Below 6) sucrose esters are more oil-soluble and are ideal for stabilizing oil-in-water emulsions. Each type caters to different formulation needs in various industries.</p></p>
