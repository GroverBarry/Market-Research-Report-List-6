<p><p>Coaxial switches are commonly classified into different types based on the number of input/output ports they have. SPnT switches have one input port and multiple output ports. SPDT switches have one input port and two output ports. DPDT switches have two input ports and two output ports. Other types of coaxial switches may include multi-port switches with more than two output ports or specialty switches with unique configurations to meet specific application requirements. Each type of switch offers different functionalities and capabilities for various applications in industries such as telecommunications, aerospace, and defense.</p></p>
