<p><ul><li>Axial Flow</li><li>Radial Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reaction turbine market can be categorized into two types based on the direction of flow of water through the turbine. Axial flow turbines have water flowing parallel to the axis of rotation, resulting in a compact design and efficient performance. Radial flow turbines have water flowing in a perpendicular direction to the axis of rotation, providing high efficiency and flexibility in various operating conditions. Both types of turbines are widely used in hydropower plants and other industrial applications for generating electricity from water flow.</p></p>
