<p><strong>The Fireproof Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired</li><li>Ceramic</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Tempered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fireproof glass is classified into four main types: wired, ceramic, laminated, and tempered. Wired fireproof glass is embedded with steel wires to prevent shattering and provide additional strength. Ceramic fireproof glass contains a layer of ceramic material that helps to withstand high temperatures. Laminated fireproof glass is made by sandwiching a layer of PVB or resin between two sheets of glass, providing impact resistance. Tempered fireproof glass is heat treated to increase its strength and resistance to thermal stress.</p></p>
