<p><p>Interesterified fats are a type of fat that is produced by rearranging the fatty acids in a triglyceride molecule. The two main types of interesterified fats are chemical interesterified fats and enzymatic interesterified fats. Chemical interesterified fats are produced using chemical catalysts to rearrange the fatty acids, while enzymatic interesterified fats are produced using enzymes to catalyze the reaction. Both types of interesterified fats have applications in the food industry as alternatives to hydrogenated fats.</p></p>
