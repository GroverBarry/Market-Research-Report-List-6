<p><p>Cold chain monitoring data loggers are essential for maintaining the quality and integrity of medical and pharmaceutical products during transport and storage. Offline data loggers are standalone devices that record temperature and humidity data for later retrieval and analysis. Cellular connected data loggers utilize a cellular network to transmit real-time monitoring data to a central system. Wireless connected data loggers use Wi-Fi or Bluetooth technology to transmit data in real-time. Each type of data logger offers different levels of convenience, accuracy, and efficiency for monitoring the cold chain.</p></p>
