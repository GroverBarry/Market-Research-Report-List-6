Sublimation printers use heat to transfer dye onto various materials, resulting in high-quality, long-lasting prints. Inkjet printers, on the other hand, use liquid ink sprayed onto paper to create prints. Both types of printers are popular in the market for their portability and convenience, allowing users to easily print images on the go. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications depending on the user's needs.</p></p>
