<p><p>Liquid particle counting systems are used to monitor and analyze the number and size of particles in a liquid. There are two main types of these systems: Offline Type and In-line Remote Type. </p><p>Offline Type systems require taking samples of the liquid and analyzing them externally in a separate location. In contrast, In-line Remote Type systems continuously monitor the liquid in real-time as it flows through a pipeline or process.</p><p>Both types of systems are crucial for ensuring the quality and cleanliness of liquids in various industries, such as pharmaceuticals, semiconductor manufacturing, and food and beverage production.</p></p>
