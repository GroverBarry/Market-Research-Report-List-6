<p><p>Solar Powered Data Buoy Market Types include Base and Tower models. Base models are designed to float on the ocean's surface and collect data using sensors located on the buoy itself. Tower models, on the other hand, are equipped with solar panels and placed on fixed structures such as offshore platforms or light towers. Tower models can withstand rough sea conditions and offer better stability than base models. Both types of buoys are important for collecting oceanographic data for various research and monitoring applications.</p></p>
