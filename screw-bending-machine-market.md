<p><p>Screw bending machines are available in three main types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual machines require physical force to bend the screws, making them suitable for small-scale operations. Electric machines are powered by electricity, offering more efficiency and precision for medium-scale projects. Hydraulic machines use hydraulic power to bend screws, making them ideal for heavy-duty industrial applications. Each type caters to different needs and scales of production, providing users with a variety of options to choose from based on their requirements.</p></p>
