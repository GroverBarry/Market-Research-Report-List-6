<p><strong>The Transformer Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bushing Monitoring</li><li>DGA Devices</li><li>Partial Discharge (PD)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transformer monitoring systems have various types, including bushing monitoring, dissolved gas analysis (DGA) devices, partial discharge (PD) monitoring, and others. Bushing monitoring systems are used to detect insulation degradation in transformer bushings. DGA devices analyze gases dissolved in transformer oil to identify potential issues. Partial discharge monitoring systems detect and locate partial discharge events in transformers. Other types of monitoring systems may include temperature sensors, moisture sensors, and vibration sensors to provide comprehensive monitoring of transformer health and performance.</p></p>
