<p><p>Reactive load banks are devices used to simulate electrical loads to test the performance of generators and electrical systems. There are three main types of reactive load banks: portable, trailer mounted, and stationary. Portable load banks are compact and easily transported, making them ideal for testing in various locations. Trailer mounted load banks are larger and often used for more heavy-duty testing requirements. Stationary load banks are permanently installed and used for continuous testing and monitoring of electrical systems. Each type offers different advantages based on the specific testing needs.</p></p>
