<p><p>Optical fluid level sensors come in two main types: contact and contactless. Contact sensors rely on physical contact with the fluid to detect the level, using probes or floats. These sensors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, healthcare, and industrial manufacturing for their accuracy and reliability in monitoring fluid levels.</p></p>
