<p><p>Personal fall protection equipment market includes various types of products such as safety harnesses, safety lanyards, anchor points, self-retracting lifelines, and other related items. Safety harnesses are critical for distributing fall impact forces and allowing workers to be suspended in a safe manner. Safety lanyards connect the harness to an anchor point, providing crucial support in case of a fall. Anchor points are fixed structures that secure the worker's safety line. Self-retracting lifelines offer automatic fall protection by retracting to prevent excess slack. Other equipment includes devices like fall arrestors and rescue systems.</p></p>
