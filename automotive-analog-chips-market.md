<p><p>Automotive Analog Chips market consists of two main types: Signal Chain Chips and Power Management Chips. Signal Chain Chips are used to process signals in a sequential manner within the automotive electronic system. They are crucial for functions like data conversion and amplification. On the other hand, Power Management Chips are responsible for managing power distribution within the system, ensuring stable and efficient power supply to all components. Both types of chips play a vital role in ensuring the smooth operation of automotive electronic systems.</p></p>
