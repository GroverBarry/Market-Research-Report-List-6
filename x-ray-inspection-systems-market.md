<p><p>X-ray Inspection Systems Market includes various types of systems such as Digital Radiography (DR) Type and Computed Tomography (CT) Type. DR Type systems use digital sensors to capture X-ray images, allowing for faster imaging and easier storage and sharing of images. CT Type systems use a combination of X-ray images taken from different angles to create a detailed 3D image of an object, providing more accurate and detailed information for inspection purposes. Both types are widely used in industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, and security.</p></p>
