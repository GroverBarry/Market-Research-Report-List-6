<p><p>The cast polymer market includes three main types: solid surface, cultured marble, and engineered stone. Solid surface materials are made from acrylic, polyester, or a combination of the two, offering a durable and non-porous surface. Cultured marble combines crushed marble with resin to create a versatile and cost-effective material. Engineered stone, such as quartz, is made from a blend of natural stone and resin, providing a strong and low-maintenance option. Each type offers unique benefits and is popular in various applications.</p></p>
