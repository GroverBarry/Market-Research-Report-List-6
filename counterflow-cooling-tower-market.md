<p><p>Counterflow cooling towers are divided into three main types: open type, closed type, and others. </p><p>Open type cooling towers are the most common, using water that is exposed to the atmosphere for cooling. Closed type cooling towers, on the other hand, uses a closed-loop system where the cooling water does not come into contact with the outside air. </p><p>The "others" category includes hybrid cooling towers that combine elements of both open and closed designs, as well as specialized cooling towers for specific applications.</p></p>
