<p><p>Gas pooling mechanisms are different market types that govern how gas resources are pooled and distributed among producers. Voluntary pooled markets allow producers to choose to pool their resources together for more efficient production. Forced pooled markets mandate that all producers in a given area contribute to a common pool. Drilling markets prioritize production from new wells. Proration markets limit production based on a pro-rata allocation system. Field enhanced recovery markets focus on maximizing recovery from existing fields. Specially defined markets are created for unique circumstances requiring specific regulations.</p></p>
