<p><p>Structured Light 3D Sensing technology is categorized into three main types: Striped Structured Light, Coded Structured Light, and Speckle Structured Light. Striped Structured Light involves projecting a patterned light onto an object and analyzing the deformation of the pattern to capture 3D data. Coded Structured Light uses a unique code pattern to accurately reconstruct a 3D model of an object. Speckle Structured Light relies on the interference patterns created by scattered light to capture 3D information with high precision and accuracy.</p></p>
