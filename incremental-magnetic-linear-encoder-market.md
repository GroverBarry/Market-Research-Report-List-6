<p><p>An Incremental Magnetic Linear Encoder is a type of linear encoder that utilizes magnetic fields to measure linear motion. It consists of a magnetic encoder strip mounted on a moving object and a magnetic read head that detects changes in the magnetic field to provide incremental position feedback. These encoders are known for their high accuracy, reliability, and durability, making them ideal for various industrial applications.</p><p>The global Incremental Magnetic Linear Encoder Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period.
