<p><p>Vacuum cryostats are commonly used for low-temperature research and experiments. There are two main types of vacuum cryostats in the market: Helium-flow and Liquid Helium cryostats. Helium-flow cryostats use a continuous flow of Helium gas to cool the sample, while Liquid Helium cryostats directly immerse the sample in a bath of liquid Helium for cooling. Both types of cryostats are essential for researchers working with ultra-low temperatures to achieve precise and reliable results in their experiments.</p></p>
