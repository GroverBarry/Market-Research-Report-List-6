<p><p>Orthodontic headgear is a dental device used to correct bite alignment issues by exerting pressure on the teeth. There are three main types of orthodontic headgear: cervical pull headgear, high-pull headgear, and reverse-pull (facemask) headgear. Cervical pull headgear is worn over the head and neck to correct overbites. High-pull headgear is used to correct underbites by pulling the upper jaw forward. Reverse-pull (facemask) headgear is used to treat underbites by pulling the upper jaw forward and the lower jaw backward.</p></p>
