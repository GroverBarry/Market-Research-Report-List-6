<p><p>Industrial tubular solenoids are electromechanical devices used to convert electrical energy into linear motion. These solenoids consist of a coil of wire wound around a metallic core, which creates a magnetic field when an electric current is passed through it. This magnetic field causes a plunger or armature to move within the solenoid, generating linear motion that can be used to operate various mechanical devices.</p><p>The Industrial Tubular Solenoids Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
