<p><p>Surgical Navigation Equipment Market includes two main types: Optical Surgical Navigation Equipment and Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation Equipment. Optical equipment uses infrared cameras to track instruments in real-time during surgery, providing precise navigation information. Electromagnetic equipment relies on magnetic fields to track the location of instruments, allowing for accurate guidance throughout the surgical procedure. Both types of equipment enhance the surgeon's precision and efficiency, leading to better patient outcomes and potentially reducing the risk of complications during surgery.</p></p>
