<p><p>Dry mortar mixers can be divided into two types: single shaft and double shaft. Single shaft dry mortar mixers mix materials in a horizontal direction, resulting in a more uniform mixture. On the other hand, double shaft dry mortar mixers mix materials in both horizontal and vertical directions, providing a more thorough and efficient mixing process. Both types of mixers are commonly used in construction projects for mixing dry powder materials such as cement, sand, and additives to create mortar for various applications.</p></p>
