<p><p>Ready-to-use therapeutic food (RUTF) comes in three main types: solid, semi-solid paste, and drinkable. Solid RUTF typically comes in bar or biscuit form and provides a convenient option for children to consume. Semi-solid paste RUTF is a thick, spreadable consistency that is easy to eat. Drinkable RUTF is a liquid form that can be easily consumed, making it suitable for infants and children who have difficulty chewing solid foods. Each type of RUTF caters to different preferences and needs, allowing for flexibility in treating malnutrition.</p></p>
