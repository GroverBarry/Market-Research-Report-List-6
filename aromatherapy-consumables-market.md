<p><p>Aromatherapy consumables market includes essential oils and carrier oils. Essential oils are highly concentrated plant extracts used in aromatherapy for their therapeutic properties. They are typically derived from flowers, leaves, roots, and fruits through distillation. Carrier oils, on the other hand, are used to dilute essential oils and help them spread over the skin during massages or topical applications. They are typically plant-based oils such as coconut, almond, or jojoba oil. These two types of oils play a crucial role in aromatherapy practices.</p></p>
