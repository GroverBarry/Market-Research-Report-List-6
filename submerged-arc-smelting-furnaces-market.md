<p><p>Submerged arc smelting furnaces are typically classified into two main types: DC submerged arc smelting furnaces and AC submerged arc smelting furnaces. DC submerged arc smelting furnaces utilize direct current for the smelting process, offering advantages such as high energy efficiency and better control over the process. On the other hand, AC submerged arc smelting furnaces use alternating current and are preferred for their ability to handle high power requirements and flexibility in operation. Both types play a crucial role in the metallurgical industry.</p></p>
