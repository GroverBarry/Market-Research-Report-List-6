<p><p>Pasteurisers are heat exchangers used to eliminate harmful bacteria in food and beverage products. Plate heat exchangers consist of thin plates with large surface areas for efficient heat transfer, ideal for smaller scale operations. Shell or tube heat exchangers have a shell with multiple tubes for heat exchange, suitable for larger volumes of products. Both market types are essential for industries like dairy and breweries to ensure product safety and quality through controlled heating processes. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the scale and requirements of the operation.</p></p>
