<p><p>RF Front End (RFFE) Filters for mobile communication consist of Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters. SAW filters are generally used in frequency ranges up to a few GHz, while BAW filters are employed in higher frequency ranges. SAW filters are cost-effective and suitable for applications like WiFi, Bluetooth, and GPS. BAW filters offer higher performance and are used in 4G, 5G, and other high-frequency applications. Both types of filters play a crucial role in improving signal quality and reducing interference in mobile devices.</p></p>
