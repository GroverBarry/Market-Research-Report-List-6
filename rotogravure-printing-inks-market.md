<p><p>Rotogravure printing inks are divided into four main types: Nitrocellulose, Polyamide, Polyurethane, and Acrylic. Nitrocellulose inks are fast-drying and ideal for printing on paper and cardboard. Polyamide inks are known for their resistance to chemicals and abrasion, making them suitable for packaging materials. Polyurethane inks offer a high level of flexibility and durability, often used for printing on plastic films. Acrylic inks provide vibrant colors and good adhesion on various substrates, commonly used for decorative printing.</p></p>
