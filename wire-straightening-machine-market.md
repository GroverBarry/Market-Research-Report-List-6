<p><p>Wire straightening machines are categorized into two main types - mechanical and numerically controlled (NC). Mechanical wire straightening machines are operated manually by adjusting mechanical settings to straighten the wire efficiently. On the other hand, numerically controlled wire straightening machines are equipped with advanced technology that allows for precise and automated control of the straightening process through computer programming. NC machines offer higher accuracy, speed, and repeatability compared to mechanical machines, making them suitable for more complex and demanding wire straightening tasks.</p></p>
