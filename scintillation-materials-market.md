<p><p>Scintillation materials are used in radiation detection and measurement. They come in three main types: solid scintillators, liquid scintillators, and gaseous scintillators. Solid scintillators are materials that emit light when charged particles interact with them. Liquid scintillators are used in applications where flexibility and transparency are important. Gaseous scintillators are ideal for high-energy radiation detection due to their low density and high sensitivity. Each type of scintillator offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
