<p><strong>The Wide-Range Vacuum Gauges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirectly Measurement</li><li>Direct Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wide-range vacuum gauges are classified into two types: Indirect Measurement and Direct Measurement. Indirect Measurement gauges rely on measuring properties influenced by gas molecules in the vacuum, such as thermal conductivity or ionization, to determine pressure. Direct Measurement gauges directly measure gas molecules in the vacuum using methods like capacitance or Pirani sensors. Indirect Measurement gauges are suitable for a wide range of pressures, while Direct Measurement gauges are more accurate but have a limited pressure range.</p></p>
