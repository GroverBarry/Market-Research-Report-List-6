<p><p>In the HVAC temperature sensor market for commercial buildings, different types of sensors are used to measure temperature accurately and efficiently. NTC temperature sensors have a negative temperature coefficient, PTC sensors have a positive temperature coefficient, RTD sensors have a resistive temperature device, and thermocouples (TCs) generate a voltage proportional to the temperature difference. Other temperature sensors may include infrared sensors, thermistors, and semiconductor sensors. Each type of sensor has its own unique characteristics and applications in the commercial HVAC market.</p></p>
