<p><p>Wireless Charging Integrated Circuits (ICs) market consists of two main types - Transmitter IC and Receiver IC. Transmitter ICs are used to generate the required magnetic field for power transfer and are typically found in charging pads or stations. Receiver ICs are used in devices like smartphones or wearable tech to receive power wirelessly, converting it into usable energy. Both types of ICs play a crucial role in enabling convenient and efficient wireless charging technology across various devices and industries.</p></p>
