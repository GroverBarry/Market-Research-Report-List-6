<p><p>Flight Propulsion Systems Market can be classified into two main types: Air Breathing Engines and Non-Air Breathing Engines. Air Breathing Engines rely on atmospheric oxygen for combustion, making them efficient for flights within Earth's atmosphere. This includes turbojet, turbofan, and ramjet engines. On the other hand, Non-Air Breathing Engines do not require atmospheric oxygen and generate thrust through alternative means such as rockets or scramjets. Both types serve different purposes in aviation and space exploration, catering to diverse requirements and technologies.</p></p>
