<p><p>Fiberglass Yarn Market consists of two main types: Fiber Yarn and Fiber Roving. Fiber Yarn is a continuous strand of fibers twisted or spun together to form a single thread, commonly used in textile and fabric production. Fiber Roving, on the other hand, is a bundle of continuous glass fibers arranged without twist, primarily used in composite materials for reinforcement. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace for their strength, durability, and thermal properties.</p></p>
