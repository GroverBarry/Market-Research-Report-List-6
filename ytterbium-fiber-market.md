<p><strong>The Ytterbium Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Clad Fiber</li><li>Double-Clad Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ytterbium fiber market can be classified into Single-Clad Fiber and Double-Clad Fiber types. Single-Clad Fiber consists of a core doped with ytterbium surrounded by a cladding material. It is primarily used for high-power amplifiers and lasers. On the other hand, Double-Clad Fiber features an additional outer cladding layer, allowing for better pump absorption efficiency and reduced photodarkening effects. This type is commonly utilized in fiber lasers for applications requiring high power output and efficient beam quality.</p></p>
