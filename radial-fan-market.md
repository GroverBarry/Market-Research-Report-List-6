<p><ul><li>Paddle Wheel</li><li>Open Wheel</li><li>Back Plate Wheel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial fans are commonly classified into three main types: Paddle Wheel, Open Wheel, and Back Plate Wheel. Paddle Wheel fans are designed with blades that resemble paddles and are typically used in applications where high air volumes are required. Open Wheel fans have an open design with straight blades, ideal for applications with low to medium air volumes. Back Plate Wheel fans feature blades attached to a back plate, allowing for efficient airflow in applications requiring high pressure or power.</p></p>
