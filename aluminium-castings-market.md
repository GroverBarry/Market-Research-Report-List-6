<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Mold Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminium castings market is divided into three main types: Die casting, Permanent mold casting, and Sand casting. Die casting involves forcing molten metal into a mold cavity under high pressure. Permanent mold casting uses reusable molds to create intricate shapes with high accuracy. Sand casting, on the other hand, uses a mold made of sand to create the desired shape. Each type of casting has its own advantages and is used in various industries depending on the requirements of the final product.</p></p>
