<p><p>Organic Canola Oil is typically produced through two main market types: Pressed and Leaching. </p><p>Pressed Canola Oil is obtained by mechanically pressing the canola seeds to extract the oil, retaining most of the nutrients and flavors of the seeds. It is considered a more natural and healthier option due to its minimal processing.</p><p>Leaching Canola Oil, on the other hand, involves using a chemical solvent to extract the oil from the seeds. This process is often quicker and more cost-effective, but may result in some loss of nutrients and flavors.</p></p>
