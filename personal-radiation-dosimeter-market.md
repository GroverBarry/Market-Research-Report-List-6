<p><p>Personal Radiation Dosimeters are devices used to measure an individual's exposure to radiation. There are several types of dosimeters available in the market, including Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD), Optically Stimulated Luminescence Dosimeters (OSL), Radio-Photoluminescent Dosimeters (RPL), and Active Dosimeters. TLD dosimeters measure radiation by recording the amount of light emitted when heated, OSL dosimeters measure radiation through light stimulation, RPL dosimeters use radioactivity to detect radiation, and Active dosimeters continuously monitor and display radiation levels in real-time. Each type serves a specific purpose in monitoring radiation exposure.</p></p>
