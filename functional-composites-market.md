<p><p>Functional composites refer to materials made from two or more constituent materials with different properties to create a single material with enhanced characteristics. Metal Matrix Composites consist of a metal matrix with reinforcements of another material like ceramic or carbon fibers, offering high strength, stiffness, and thermal conductivity. Polymer Matrix Composites use a polymer matrix with reinforcing fibers like glass or carbon to provide lightweight, corrosion resistance, and flexibility. Both types have various applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
