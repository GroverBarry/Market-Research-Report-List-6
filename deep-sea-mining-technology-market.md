Manganese nodules are small, rock-like formations found on the seabed that contain valuable metals like manganese and nickel. Seafloor massive sulphides are deposits rich in minerals like copper, gold, and silver. These markets are being explored and developed for their potential economic and industrial benefits.</p></p>
