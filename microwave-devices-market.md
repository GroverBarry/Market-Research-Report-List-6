<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Microwave Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Microwave Devices</li><li>Passive Microwave Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active microwave devices refer to products that require an external power source to amplify or modify microwave signals, such as microwave transistors or amplifiers. Passive microwave devices, on the other hand, do not require an external power source and instead rely on the inherent properties of the materials used to manipulate microwave signals, such as filters or couplers. Both markets cater to different needs within the microwave industry, with active devices providing amplification and signal processing while passive devices focus on signal manipulation and control.</p></p>
