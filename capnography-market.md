Main-stream capnographs have sensors directly attached to the patient's airway, side-stream capnographs collect a sample from the patient's airway through a tube, and micro-stream capnographs use a small sensor that is placed in the patient's nasal prongs. The capnography disposables market includes various accessories and components necessary for the proper functioning of capnography devices.</p></p>
