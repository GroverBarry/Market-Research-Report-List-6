<p><p>The lawn leaf sweeper market can be divided into two main types: push-along sweepers and tow-behind sweepers. Push-along sweepers are manually operated machines that are pushed by the user to collect leaves and debris from the lawn. They are typically more affordable and easier to maneuver in smaller spaces. On the other hand, tow-behind sweepers are attached to a riding lawn mower or tractor and are ideal for larger yards or properties. They are more efficient at collecting larger quantities of leaves and debris.</p></p>
