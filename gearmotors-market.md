Helical-bevel gearmotors provide a combination of strength and precision. Planetary gearmotors are compact and offer high torque capacity. Worm gearmotors are known for their quiet operation and high reduction ratios. Other types of gearmotors may include spur, bevel, or parallel shaft designs.
