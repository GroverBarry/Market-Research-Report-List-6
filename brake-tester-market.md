<p><p>Brake tester market types include roller brake testers, plate brake testers, and decelerometers. Roller brake testers use rotating rollers to simulate road conditions and test the brakes by applying resistance. Plate brake testers measure a vehicle's braking force by pressing brake plates against the wheels. Decelerometers measure a vehicle's deceleration rate to determine brake performance. Each type of brake tester offers unique advantages and is used in various automotive testing scenarios to ensure the safety and efficiency of brake systems.</p></p>
