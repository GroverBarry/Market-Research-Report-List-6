<p><p>Aerospace & Defense Carbon Fiber Composite market can be divided into two main types - Thermosetting Type and Thermoplastic Type. Thermosetting Type composites are rigid and brittle materials that are cured under a specific temperature and pressure. They offer high strength and stiffness, making them suitable for structural applications in aerospace and defense industry. On the other hand, Thermoplastic Type composites are more flexible and can be reshaped when heated, offering ease of processing and recyclability, making them suitable for components that require forming or reshaping.</p></p>
