<p><p>Antibody library technology has revolutionized the discovery of novel antibodies for therapeutic purposes. Different types of display technologies are used to present a diverse array of antibodies for screening. Phage display utilizes bacteriophages to display antibodies, while ribosome display directly links the genotype and phenotype of antibodies. Yeast display utilizes yeast cells to present antibodies on their surface, and mammalian cell surface display involves expressing antibodies on the surface of mammalian cells. These technologies have significantly accelerated the generation of high-affinity antibodies for various applications.</p></p>
