<p><strong>The Fuel Injection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI)</li><li>Mechanical Fuel Injection System (MFI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel Injection Equipment Market consists of two main types: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) and Mechanical Fuel Injection System (MFI). EFI utilizes electronic sensors to precisely control the fuel delivery to the engine, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and performance. On the other hand, MFI uses mechanical processes such as fuel pumps and injectors to deliver fuel into the engine. While EFI is more common in modern vehicles due to its efficiency and precision, MFI is still preferred in some high-performance applications for its simplicity and reliability.</p></p>
