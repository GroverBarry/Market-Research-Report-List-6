<p><p>Sunflower oil is classified into three main types based on their oleic acid content: linoleic oil, mid-oleic oil, and high-oleic oil. Linoleic oil has the lowest oleic acid content, while mid-oleic oil has a medium oleic acid content. High-oleic oil contains the highest oleic acid content. Each type of sunflower oil is used for different purposes in the market, with high-oleic oil being preferred for its stability and health benefits, while linoleic oil is commonly used for frying and cooking.</p></p>
