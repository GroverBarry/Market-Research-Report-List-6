<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services market consists of various testing methods such as Ultrasonic Testing, Radiography Testing, Magnetic Particle Testing, Liquid Penetrant Testing, and other methods. Ultrasonic Testing involves sending sound waves through materials to detect defects. Radiography Testing uses x-rays or gamma rays to inspect the internal structure of objects. Magnetic Particle Testing detects surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials. Liquid Penetrant Testing involves applying a dye to detect surface defects. Other NDT methods include eddy current testing, visual inspection, and infrared thermography.</p></p>
