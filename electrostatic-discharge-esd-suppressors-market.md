<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Suppressors are devices used to protect electronic components from damage caused by electrostatic discharge. There are three main types of ESD suppressors: Diode Array, Polymer, and TVS. Diode Array suppressors use multiple diodes to divert excess voltage away from sensitive components. Polymer suppressors utilize polymer materials to absorb and dissipate electrostatic discharge. TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) suppressors provide fast response times to divert harmful voltage spikes. Each type offers unique advantages and suits different applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
