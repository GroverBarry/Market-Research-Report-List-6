<p><p>Styrene Butadiene Styrene (SBS) is a synthetic rubber widely used in various industries. It is available in two main types - oil-extended SBS and non-oil-extended SBS. Oil-extended SBS is produced by blending SBS with rubber extenders such as oils to improve its flexibility and durability. On the other hand, non-oil-extended SBS is manufactured without the use of oils, making it ideal for applications where oil contamination is a concern. Both types offer unique properties and advantages depending on the specific requirements of the end-use applications.</p></p>
