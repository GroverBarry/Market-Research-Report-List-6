<p><p>The Bottle Case Packer market is segmented into three types based on the level of automation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual case packers require human operators to load and pack bottles into cases. Semi-automatic packers involve some level of automation, such as case erecting or sealing, but still require human intervention for bottle placement. Fully automatic packers are completely automated, from case erecting to bottle packaging, reducing the need for human labor and increasing efficiency in the packaging process.</p></p>
