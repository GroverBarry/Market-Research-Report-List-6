<p><strong>The Contour Measuring Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Measuring Machine</li><li>Non-contact Measuring Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact measuring machines use physical probes to make direct contact with the object being measured, while non-contact measuring machines use technologies such as lasers or cameras to measure objects without touching them. Contact measuring machines are typically used for high-precision measurements where surface contact is necessary, while non-contact measuring machines are often used for delicate or fragile objects where physical contact could damage the object. Both types have their own strengths and weaknesses depending on the specific application.</p></p>
