<p><p>Heating modules in the market can be categorized into two main types: metallic and ceramic fiber modules. Metallic heating modules are constructed using materials such as stainless steel or nickel-chromium alloys, which offer high heat resistance and durability. On the other hand, ceramic fiber heating modules utilize insulation materials like alumina-silica fibers, providing excellent thermal insulation properties. Both types of heating modules are widely used in industrial applications for processes such as drying, curing, and heat treatment due to their efficient heat transfer capabilities.</p></p>
