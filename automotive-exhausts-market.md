<p><p>The automotive exhausts market offers different types of emission control technologies such as Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC), Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF), Lean Nox Trap (LNT), and Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR). DOC helps in oxidizing carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons, while DPF captures and removes particulate matter. LNT reduces nitrogen oxides, and SCR converts nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water vapor through a catalytic process. These technologies play a crucial role in reducing harmful emissions from vehicles and promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
