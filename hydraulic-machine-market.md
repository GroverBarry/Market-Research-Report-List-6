Different types of hydraulic machines include Movable Table Hydraulic Press, Movable Frame Hydraulic Press, Air Operated Hydraulic Press, and Double Acting Hydraulic Press. Movable Table Hydraulic Presses have a movable table for flexibility, while Movable Frame Hydraulic Presses have a movable frame for versatility. Air Operated Hydraulic Presses use compressed air for power, and Double Acting Hydraulic Presses have a hydraulic cylinder that operates in both directions for increased efficiency.</p></p>
