<p><p>Inverter and Constant Frequency are two main types of commercial central air conditioning systems in the market. Inverter systems use variable speed technology to adjust the compressor speed based on the cooling demand, resulting in energy efficiency and precise temperature control. Constant Frequency systems, on the other hand, have a fixed compressor speed and operate at a consistent rate regardless of the cooling needs. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications depending on the requirements of the space.</p></p>
