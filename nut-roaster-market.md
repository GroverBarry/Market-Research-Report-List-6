<p><ul><li>Hot-Air Type</li><li>Direct-Fire Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nut roasters can be classified into two main types: hot-air type and direct-fire type. Hot-air type nut roasters use heated air to roast the nuts evenly, resulting in a consistent and high-quality end product. On the other hand, direct-fire type nut roasters roast nuts using direct heat from a flame, which can provide a more intense flavor but may require closer monitoring to prevent burning. Both types offer unique advantages and are popular choices in the nut roaster market.</p></p>
