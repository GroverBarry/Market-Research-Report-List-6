<p><p>Digital Production Printers are segmented into two main types: Inkjet Digital Production Printers and Electrophotography Digital Production Printers.</p><p>Inkjet Digital Production Printers use droplets of ink to create images on paper or other media, providing high-quality color printing and flexibility for a variety of applications.</p><p>Electrophotography Digital Production Printers, on the other hand, use a laser to create an electrostatic image on a drum, which is then transferred to paper and fused with heat. This technology is known for its speed and precision in creating high-volume prints.</p></p>
