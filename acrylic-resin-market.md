<p><strong>The Acrylic Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Acrylic Resin</li><li>Thermoplastic Acrylic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic resin market can be divided into two main types - thermosetting and thermoplastic acrylic resins. Thermosetting acrylic resins are materials that undergo a chemical reaction during curing, forming a permanent bond that cannot be melted or reshaped. On the other hand, thermoplastic acrylic resins can be melted and reshaped multiple times without affecting their properties. Both types have their own set of applications and advantages, catering to different needs in various industries.</p></p>
