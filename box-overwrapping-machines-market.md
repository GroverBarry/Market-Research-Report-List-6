<p><p>Box overwrapping machines are classified into three types based on their operating mechanism - manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require operators to manually feed, wrap, and seal boxes. Semi-automatic machines partially automate the wrapping process, requiring manual intervention for certain tasks. Automatic machines are fully automated and can wrap, seal, and label boxes without human intervention. The choice of machine type depends on the volume of boxes to be wrapped and the level of automation desired by the user.</p></p>
