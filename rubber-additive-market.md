<p><ul><li>Antidegradants</li><li>Accelerators</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber additives can be categorized into different types based on their functions. Antidegradants are additives that help protect rubber from degradation due to environmental factors such as heat, light, and oxygen. Accelerators are substances that speed up the vulcanization process of rubber, allowing for faster production of rubber products. Other types of additives in the rubber market include plasticizers, activators, and fillers, which can enhance various properties of rubber such as flexibility, strength, and durability. These additives play a crucial role in improving the performance and longevity of rubber products.</p></p>
