The PA non-woven wheels are known for their high strength and abrasion resistance, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. PP non-woven wheels are characterized by their flexibility and chemical resistance, making them ideal for use in polishing and finishing tasks. PE non-woven wheels offer excellent impact resistance and durability. Other materials used in non-woven wheels include polyester and nylon, each offering unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
