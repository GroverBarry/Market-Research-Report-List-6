<p><p>The Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia Devices market includes various types of procedures for treating an enlarged prostate. Transurethral Needle Ablation of the Prostate (TUNA) uses radiofrequency energy to reduce prostate tissue. Laser Surgery involves vaporizing or cutting excess tissue. Prostatic Stenting involves placing a stent to keep the urethra open. Others procedures include minimally invasive techniques for treating BPH.</p></p>
