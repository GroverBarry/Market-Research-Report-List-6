<p><strong>The Optical Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)</li><li>Photoacoustic Imaging</li><li>Diffused Optical Tomography</li><li>Hyperspectral Imaging</li><li>Near-Infrared Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical imaging encompasses various market types, including Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT), which provides high-resolution cross-sectional images of tissues. Photoacoustic Imaging combines optical and ultrasound techniques for deep tissue imaging. Diffuse Optical Tomography uses near-infrared light to image tissues in 3D. Hyperspectral Imaging captures images at multiple wavelengths to analyze material properties. Near-Infrared Spectroscopy measures the absorption of near-infrared light to assess oxygenation levels in tissues. Each of these optical imaging technologies offers unique advantages in medical diagnostics and research.</p></p>
