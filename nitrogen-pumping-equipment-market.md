<p><p>The Nitrogen Pumping Equipment Market includes two main types of pumps: Triplex Pump and Quintuplex Pump. Triplex pumps have three plungers and are typically used for applications that require lower pressures and flow rates. On the other hand, Quintuplex pumps have five plungers and are more suitable for applications that demand higher pressures and flow rates. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in nitrogen pumping applications across various industries such as oil and gas, mining, and agriculture.</p></p>
