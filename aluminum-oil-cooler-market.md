<p><strong>The Aluminum Oil Cooler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pass</li><li>Dual Pass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminum Oil Cooler market is categorized into two main types: Single Pass and Dual Pass. In a Single Pass system, the oil flows through the cooler in one continuous path, providing efficient cooling but limited contact time with the cooler. On the other hand, a Dual Pass system allows the oil to pass through the cooler twice, increasing the cooling efficiency and allowing for better heat dissipation. Both types offer unique benefits and are widely used in various automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
