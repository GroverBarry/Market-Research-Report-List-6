<p><p>Resistance welding services comprise various types, such as spot welding, projection welding, seam welding, butt welding, and resistance bolt welding. Spot welding involves joining two metal surfaces at specific points, projection welding is used for welding nuts or studs onto a metal surface, seam welding creates a continuous weld along a seam, butt welding joins two pieces end-to-end, and resistance bolt welding is used for welding bolts onto metal surfaces. Each type caters to specific welding requirements in different industries.</p></p>
