<p><p>Arrayed Waveguide Gratings (AWGs) are key components in wavelength division multiplexing (WDM) communication systems. There are two types of AWGs - Thermal AWG and Athermal AWG. Thermal AWGs require temperature stabilization to maintain peak performance, making them suitable for controlled environments. On the other hand, Athermal AWGs eliminate the need for temperature control, providing stability over a wider range of temperatures. Each type of AWG caters to different market needs, allowing for greater flexibility in designing and implementing WDM systems.</p></p>
