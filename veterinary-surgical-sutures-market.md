<p><p>Veterinary Surgical Sutures Market Types include absorbable sutures and non-absorbable sutures. Absorbable sutures are designed to break down and be absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal. They are commonly used for internal stitches and in tissues that heal quickly. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, are not broken down by the body and require removal after the wound has healed. They are typically used for external stitches and in tissues that heal slowly.</p></p>
