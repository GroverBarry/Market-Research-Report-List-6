<p><p>A Polycrystalline Diamond Compact (PDC) Bit is a type of drill bit used in the oil and gas industry to drill through hard rock formations. PDC bits offer improved penetration rates, longer lifespan, and reduced downtime compared to traditional roller cone bits.</p><p>The Polycrystalline Diamond Compact (PDC) Bit Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period.
