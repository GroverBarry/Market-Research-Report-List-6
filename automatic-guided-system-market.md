<p><p>Automatic Guided Systems can be categorized into three main types based on the technology they use for guidance: magnetic, electronic, and laser. </p><p>Magnetic AGVs rely on magnetic tape or markers on the floor for navigation, while Electronic AGVs use wires embedded in the floor for guidance. Laser AGVs, on the other hand, use laser technology to map their environment and navigate without physical markers. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different applications in industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and healthcare.</p></p>
