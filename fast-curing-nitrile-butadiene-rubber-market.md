<p><p>Fast Curing Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR) is commonly categorized into two types based on the curing process: Cold Curing NBR and Hot Curing NBR. Cold Curing NBR utilizes lower temperatures and longer curing times, making it suitable for applications where heat resistance is not critical. On the other hand, Hot Curing NBR requires higher temperatures and shorter curing times, resulting in improved heat resistance properties. Both types offer fast curing capabilities, but their suitability depends on specific application requirements.</p></p>
