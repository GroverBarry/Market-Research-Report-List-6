<p><p>Mechanical vacuum pumps are categorized into various types such as piston pump, diaphragm pump, scroll pump, roots pump, screw pump, claw type pump, rotary vane pump, turbomolecular pump, and others. Piston pump uses a piston to create a vacuum, while diaphragm pump uses a flexible diaphragm to pump fluids. Scroll pump, roots pump, screw pump, claw type pump, and rotary vane pump each have unique mechanisms to create a vacuum. Turbomolecular pump uses high-speed rotating blades to pump gas. Others include different variations and combinations of these pumping mechanisms.</p></p>
