<p><p>Facial treatment masks come in two main types: flake masks and paste masks. Flake masks are typically dry formulations that are applied to the skin, left to dry, and then peeled off, removing impurities and dead skin cells. Paste masks, on the other hand, are creamy or gel-like formulations that are applied to the skin and left on for a period of time before being rinsed off, providing deep hydration and nourishment. Both types of masks aim to improve the appearance and health of the skin.</p></p>
