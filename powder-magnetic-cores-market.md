<p><p>Powder magnetic cores are typically categorized into different types based on the material used in their construction. These types include MPP, Sendust, High Flux, Fe-Si, and Others. MPP cores are known for their high permeability and low core losses. Sendust cores offer a balance of magnetic properties and cost-effectiveness. High Flux cores provide high saturation flux density. Fe-Si cores are commonly used for high frequency applications. Other types of powder magnetic cores offer a range of unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
