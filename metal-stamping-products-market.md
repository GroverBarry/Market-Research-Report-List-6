<p><strong>The Metal Stamping Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blanking Process</li><li>Embossing Process</li><li>Bending Process</li><li>Coining Process</li><li>Flanging Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal stamping products market includes various processes such as blanking, embossing, bending, coining, and flanging. Blanking process involves cutting out a flat shape from a metal sheet. Embossing process creates raised or sunken designs on the surface of the metal. Bending process involves bending the metal sheet to a desired shape. Coining process is used to create detailed and precise designs on the metal surface. Flanging process involves bending the edges of the metal sheet to form a flange. Each process caters to different requirements in the metal stamping industry.</p></p>
