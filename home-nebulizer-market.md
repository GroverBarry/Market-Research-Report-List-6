<p><p>The home nebulizer market offers various types of nebulizers including compression atomizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, and net atomizers. Compression atomizers utilize compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation. Ultrasonic nebulizers use high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist for treatment. Net atomizers, also known as mesh nebulizers, use a vibrating mesh to create a consistent particle size for efficient inhalation. Each type of nebulizer offers unique features and benefits for individuals with respiratory conditions.</p></p>
