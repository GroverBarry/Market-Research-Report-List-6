<p><p>Entrained bed gasifier is a type of gasification process utilized to convert biomass into syngas. This market includes fixed bed and fluidized bed gasifiers. In fixed bed gasifiers, the biomass is stationary and gasification reactions occur at high temperatures. On the other hand, fluidized bed gasifiers suspend the biomass in a bed of inert materials, allowing for better mixing and heat transfer. Both types of gasifiers have their own advantages and applications, with fixed bed gasifiers being more suitable for smaller scale operations and fluidized bed gasifiers being ideal for larger scale industrial applications.</p></p>
