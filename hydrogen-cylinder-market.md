Type I cylinders are made of steel, Type II cylinders have a steel liner with a carbon fiber composite wrap, Type III cylinders have a seamless aluminum liner reinforced with carbon fiber, Type IV cylinders consist of a plastic liner wrapped in carbon fiber, and Type V cylinders are fully made of composite materials. Each type offers different advantages in terms of weight, capacity, and safety.</p></p>
