<p><p>Flex-Rigid PCB market consists of three types: Single Layer, Double Layer, and Multi-Layer. Single Layer PCBs have a single layer of substrate material, suitable for simple applications. Double Layer PCBs have two layers of substrate connected by vias, providing more flexibility and functionality. Multi-Layer PCBs have multiple layers of substrate, allowing for complex designs and higher component density. Each type offers different levels of flexibility, functionality, and complexity to cater to a wide range of electronic applications.</p></p>
