<p><p>Sheet metal forming machines are available in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require the operator to manually feed and manipulate the metal, making them suitable for small-scale operations. Semi-automatic machines offer some degree of automation in feeding and forming processes, improving productivity and efficiency. Automatic machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention and suitable for high-volume production. Each type offers varying levels of efficiency, productivity, and precision to cater to different application requirements.</p></p>
