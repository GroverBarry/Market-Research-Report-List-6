<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile inks are used in various printing methods to create designs and patterns on fabrics. Reactive dye inks are known for their vibrant colors and ability to bond with fabric fibers. Acidic inks are used for printing on silk and wool due to their strong adherence to protein-based fibers. Paint inks are commonly used in screen printing and provide a bold and opaque finish. Dispersion inks are water-based inks that offer good color density and wash resistance. Sublimation inks are used in heat transfer printing for synthetic fabrics.</p></p>
