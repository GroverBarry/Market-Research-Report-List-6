<p><strong>The Ancillary Lithography Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Developer</li><li>Remover</li><li>Stripping Agent</li><li>Edge Bead Solvent</li><li>Cleaning Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ancillary lithography materials market includes various types of products essential for the lithography process in semiconductor manufacturing. Developer is used to create patterns on a photoresist-coated substrate, while Remover removes excess photoresist after the development process. Stripping agents are utilized to completely remove photoresist layers. Edge bead solvents help control the spreading of photoresist at the edges. Cleaning products are used to maintain the cleanliness of equipment and ensure high-quality lithography results. Together, these products support the lithography process in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
