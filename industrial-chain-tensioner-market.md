<p><p>Industrial chain tensioners are used to maintain proper tension in chains used in various industries. The three main types of chain tensioners are floating, roll, and arm style. Floating chain tensioners are designed to move with the chain, while roll tensioners use a rolling mechanism to adjust tension. Arm style tensioners use an arm to apply pressure on the chain. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the industrial application.</p></p>
