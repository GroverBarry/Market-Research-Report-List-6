Medium carbon steel has a carbon content ranging from 0.3% to 0.6%, offering a balance of strength and ductility for applications like automotive parts and machinery. High carbon steel contains more than 0.6% carbon, providing exceptional hardness and wear resistance, ideal for cutting tools and knives.</p></p>
