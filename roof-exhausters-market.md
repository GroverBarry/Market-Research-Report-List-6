<p><p>Roof exhausters are commonly classified into two types: vertical axial exhausters and square tapered exhausters. Vertical axial exhausters are designed to expel air vertically from the building, providing efficient ventilation for larger spaces. On the other hand, square tapered exhausters are ideal for removing air horizontally from the building, offering a more targeted ventilation solution for specific areas. Both types of roof exhausters play a vital role in maintaining indoor air quality and promoting a comfortable environment within commercial and industrial buildings.</p></p>
