<p><p>Rubber track pads for excavators come in three main types: bolt-on, clip-on, and chain-on. Bolt-on track pads are securely attached to the excavator's steel tracks with bolts, providing a strong and durable connection. Clip-on track pads are easy to install and remove, ideal for temporary use or frequent replacement. Chain-on track pads utilize chains to secure the pads onto the tracks, offering flexibility and ease of installation. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the construction industry.</p></p>
